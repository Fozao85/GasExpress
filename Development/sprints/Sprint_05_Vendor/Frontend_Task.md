# Frontend — Vendor Portal

## Screens
- [ ] Business Verification — Submit business documents, upload logo, verification status
- [ ] Pending Approval — Waiting screen with estimated review time
- [ ] Dashboard — KPI cards (active orders, revenue, stock alerts), top products chart
- [ ] Orders — Tab filter (Active/History), order cards, order detail with timeline + status actions
- [ ] Inventory — Category tabs, stock cards with low-stock warning
- [ ] Revenue — Revenue chart (AreaChart), summary, recent transactions
- [ ] Profile — Business info, verification status, business details
