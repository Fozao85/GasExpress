# Sprint 05 Acceptance Criteria

## Vendor Registration

Vendor can:

- Create account
- Submit business information
- Upload verification documents

---

## Verification

System supports:

- Pending approval
- Approved vendor
- Rejected vendor

---

## Inventory

Vendor can:

- View products
- Update stock
- Update prices
- Change availability

---

## Orders

Vendor can:

- Receive incoming orders
- Accept orders (moves to Preparing)
- Reject orders (removed from active queue)
- Mark as Preparing
- Mark as Ready for Pickup
- Mark as Completed
- Order lifecycle enforced: Incoming → Accept → Preparing → Ready → Picked Up → Completed
- Invalid transitions rejected

---

## Revenue

Vendor can:

- View earnings
- View transactions
- View reports

---

## Security

System ensures:

- Vendors only access their own business data
- Only approved vendors operate

---

# Definition Of Success

Sprint 05 is complete when LPG vendors can manage their business operations through the GasNow Vendor Application.
