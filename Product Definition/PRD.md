# Product Definition

## Product Name (Working)

**GasNow** *(Other options: GasGo, GasExpress, MyGas, QuickGas, GasLink)*

---

# Product Vision

GasNow is an on-demand marketplace that connects customers who need cooking gas with nearby verified gas vendors and delivery riders, enabling fast, reliable, and transparent home delivery of LPG cylinders.

The platform digitizes the traditional process of buying cooking gas by allowing customers to browse vendors, compare prices, place orders, track deliveries, and pay securely—all from a mobile app.

---

# Problem Statement

Purchasing cooking gas is often inconvenient and unpredictable. Customers frequently discover their gas has run out unexpectedly, requiring them to spend valuable time calling multiple vendors, arranging transportation, or traveling with empty cylinders.

Gas vendors have limited digital visibility and rely heavily on walk-in customers, while delivery riders lack a centralized platform for receiving delivery requests.

GasNow solves this by creating a real-time marketplace where customers, vendors, and riders can interact efficiently.

## Additional Recommendations

To make GasNow stand out from existing delivery platforms, consider these differentiators over time:

* **Cylinder exchange support:** Many customers want to exchange an empty cylinder for a filled one rather than buying a new cylinder outright. Make this a core order option.
* **Emergency delivery:** Offer an express delivery tier for customers who need gas as quickly as possible.
* **Vendor stock visibility:** Show only vendors with confirmed inventory to reduce canceled orders.
* **Safety-first experience:** Include delivery safety guidelines, vendor verification badges, and rider identity verification to build trust.
* **Predictive reminders:** With user consent, analyze ordering history to remind customers before they run out of gas.

This PRD defines a clear MVP while leaving room for future growth into a comprehensive LPG delivery marketplace.
